# Content Review

Agent pack for managing a knowledge base. Coordinates researchers, editors,
and fact-checkers to produce documentation — runbooks, guides, playbooks,
and research summaries.

## Roles

- **manager** — Triages incoming issues, dispatches work to specialists
- **researcher** — Investigates topics, produces structured research summaries
- **editor** — Writes and edits content for clarity and accuracy
- **fact_checker** — Verifies claims against source material
